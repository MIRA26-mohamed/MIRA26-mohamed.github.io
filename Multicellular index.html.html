• Multicellular organisms

    • living organisms characterized by the following characteristics:

  • Their body consist of many cells, so they are called multicellular organisms.

  • Their cells are are differentiated, meaning that they vary in their shape and structure.

  • Their cells are specialized in their function, meaning that each group of cells performs specific vital processes.
  • Relatively larg in size and can be seen by the naked eye.
